    Hi, I am Eston Livingston. I am a young, up and coming graphic designer in the greater Detroit, Michigan area. I am currently attending school at Specs Howard School of Media Arts to earn my degree in Graphic Design. I have always been creative growing up and at first my passion was reading, and writing stories of my own. It was not until my sophmore year of high school that I truly discovered my love of imagery art. I learned how to paint with oil paints and was taught by my step grandfather John Reed. He instilled in me a love of imagery. What I originally loved to write about, I was now able to create with a paintbrush. I was then led by my love of technology to use my new found love of imagery along side technology and combine them, so here I am.

    I am creative by God, but a designer by choice.
